| OUR STORY

The IFSA Journey

Founded with the vision of spreading the ancient art of Shito-Ryu Karate across India, the Indian Fit-Sports Academy has grown from a single dojo into a multi-location training institution that has produced hundreds of dedicated martial artists.

Affiliated with the Ichiban Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Organization India (ISKO), we follow the traditional teachings while adapting them for modern practitioners. Our senseis bring decades of competitive experience and teaching expertise to every class.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an advanced competitor, IFSA provides the structure, guidance, and community you need to reach your potential.

| CORE VALUES

The IFSA Philosophy

We live by three core principles inside and outside the dojo.

Discipline

Motivation gets you started. Discipline keeps you going. We teach students to control their mind and body.

Respect

We bow when we enter, we bow when we leave. Respect for the sensei, for opponents, and for oneself is non-negotiable.

Excellence

We don't aim for perfection; we aim for progress. Every drop of sweat is a step towards mastery.

| RECOGNIZED BY

Our Official Affiliations

We are proud to be recognized by the highest governing bodies in Karate, ensuring our students receive globally certified training.

Karate India Organization (KIO)

The only National Federation for Karate in India recognized by the World Karate Federation (WKF).

Includes ISKO affiliation for school-level competitions.

ISKO

Indian Schools Karate Organization — governing school-level competitions and youth development.

World Karate Federation (WKF)

The largest international governing body of sport karate with over 190 member countries.

Asian Karate Federation (AKF)

The governing body of karate in Asia, organizing major continental championships.
