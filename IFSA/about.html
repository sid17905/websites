Our Origin

It Started With a Vision

IFSA was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to make world-class martial arts training accessible to everyone. We noticed that many gyms focused only on "fighting," but lost the essence of "art."

We combine the traditional discipline of Ichiban Shito-Ryu Karate with modern sports science. Whether you are 5 years old or 50, our dojo is a place where you push your limits safely.

The IFSA Philosophy

We live by three core principles inside and outside the dojo.

Discipline

Motivation gets you started. Discipline keeps you going. We teach students to control their mind and body.

Respect

We bow when we enter, we bow when we leave. Respect for the sensei, for opponents, and for oneself is non-negotiable.

Excellence

We don't aim for perfection; we aim for progress. Every drop of sweat is a step towards mastery.
